Ginger juice

GIngredients: 1 liter of water 1 lemon 1 ginger root Preparation

1. The preparation is quite simple, pour the liter of water into a saucepan and bring it to medium heat. Meanwhile wash the ginger well and grate the root, once the water is boiled add the grated ginger, cover the saucepan tightly and let it boil for 10 minutes.

2. Meanwhile wash the lemon well and cut into thin slices, after 10 minutes open the saucepan and add the lemons, turn off the heat, cover the pot and let it rest for about 10 minutes.

3. Ready you can taste your rich and delicious ginger juice with lemon, add honey if you like to sweeten a little.
